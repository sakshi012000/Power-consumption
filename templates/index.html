Power Consumption Data Analysis for year 2019 and 2020 in India.

This is a analysis of power consumption by the states of india in year 2019 and 2020. The visualization is done with the help of Matplotlib and seaborn. The frontend is done with HTML , javascript and CSS and backend is done by the flask framework.

Average Consumption

This bar plot is made showing the average power consumption by each state during year 2019 and 2020.The plot shows that Maharashtra is the highest average power consumer followed by Gujarat and UP.



Region wise power Consumption

This line graph shows the power consumed by regions viz NR , SR , ER , WR , NER of india. This graph clearly depicts that the average power consumed by a state in West region is maximum and in North East Region is minimum.



Average consumption in a state of a Particular Region.

This pie chart shows the average power consumption in a states of a region compared to the average power consumption of states of another region . From this chart we can see that average power consumption in WR states is maximum compared to other region and is minimum for states of North Eastern Region.



Annual power consumption 2019

This bar plot shows the total consumption of power in year 2019 by different states. From the graph we can say that maharashtra has maximum annual consumption followed by gujarat and UP in the year 2019. The Y axis depicts the total sum of consumption by the corresponding state.



Annual power consumption 2020

This bar plot shows the total consumption of power in year 2020 by different states. From the graph we can say that maharashtra has maximum annual consumption followed by gujarat and UP in the year 2020. The Y axis depicts the total sum of consumption by the corresponding state.



Correlation matrix for Regions.

This is a correlation heatmap showing relation between the power usage by different regions.



Power consumption variation in different regions.

This graph shows the nature of variation of power usage in different regions with respect to time . We can also conclude that Norther region has maximum total power consumption on most days and the power consumption and its variation in North Eastern region is not very significant witn respect to other regions.



Power Consumption before and after Lockdown

In this bar plot equal amount of days are taken before and after lockdown so as to analyze the change in consumption in each state .From graph we can see that power consumption has increased slightly in MP , Rajasthan etc. and is decreased in Punjab , UP etc.



Geographic Map Plot

This map is drawn with the help of latitude and longitude data and it shows the power usage in different regions. Density of usage is also clearly depicted in this map.
